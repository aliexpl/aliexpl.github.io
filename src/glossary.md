# Glossary

Here are the definitions of key terms used in this book.

**Ghost:** The spirit of a dead person, especially one that is believed to appear
to the living in bodily form or to haunt specific locations.
[↗](https://www.thefreedictionary.com/ghost)

**Jump scare:** In a movie or video game, a sudden movement, appearance, or loud sound meant to startle the viewer.
[↗](https://www.dictionary.com/browse/jump-scare)

**Paranormal:** Beyond the range of normal experience or scientific explanation.
[↗](https://www.thefreedictionary.com/paranormal)

**Pareidolia:** The perception of a recognizable image or meaningful pattern where none exists or is intended.
[↗](https://www.thefreedictionary.com/Pareidolia)

**Spirit:** A force or principle believed to animate living beings.
[↗](https://www.thefreedictionary.com/spirit)
