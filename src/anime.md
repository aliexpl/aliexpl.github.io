## Mieruko-chan [[↗]](https://myanimelist.net/anime/48483/Mieruko-chan)

One of the good things about being able to see and hear spirits is that you can
help them resolve unfinished business.

Like in episode 5, where the husband's spirit tells Miko the safe's password,
and Miko types it into her phone and shows it to the wife after she has tried multiple times to open it.
