# Book

## Investigating Ghosts: The Scientific Search for Spirits

Benjamin Radford. 2017.

"This book is a guide to science-based ghost investigation."

Guidelines for Skeptical Paranormal Investigation:
1. Focus on relevant issues.
2. Establish the claims clearly.
3. The devil is in the details.
4. Questions will sometimes remain after proposing a solution.
5. The burden of proof is on the person making the claim, not on the investigator to disprove it.
6. Follow the evidence objectively to its conslusion. Don't try to prove or disprove it.
7. Do sufficient background research on the topic.
9. Recognize that investigations often draw upon a wide variety of disciplines.
10. Demand specifity and clarity from people about their claims.
11. Seek primary sources and documents.
12. Get a wide variety of claims about the topic.
13. Look for implications and hidden assumptions underlying claims.
14. Keep calm, especially when conducting field investigation.
15. Look for the social context of a claim.
16. Re-creations and re-enactments can be very helpful in trying to determine what happened, and how.
18. Look not only for positive evidence but also for negative evidence.
19. Whenever possible, construct timelines for events.
20. Treat eyewitnesses and claimants with respect.
