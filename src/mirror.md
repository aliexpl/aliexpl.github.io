# Mirror

Type: visual.

## Design 1

**Goal**: To record a ghost in the mirror.

**Place**: Stigmatized building with a bathroom, washbasin, and mirror.

**Time**: Night.

**Gear**: Smart glasses with camera. LED lantern. Tissue.

**Assumption**:
- The building is safe.
- There is a nice surface to place the lantern on.
- A ghost won't appear if there is camera.
- A ghost doesn't know that your smart glasses have camera.

**Step**:
Do this at 18:30, 20:00, 00:00, 02:00, 04:00.

1. Enter the bathroom.
2. Place the lantern in a stable position.
3. Go to the washbasin and look in the mirror.
4. Wipe your face with tissue while looking down.
5. Look again at the mirror.
6. Repeat steps 4 and 5 five times.
